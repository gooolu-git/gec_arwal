About

Government Engineering College (GEC), Arwal, is a premier institution established by the Government of Bihar under the Department of Science, Technology, and Technical Education. Since its inaugural academic session in 2019, GEC Arwal has made significant strides in offering quality technical education. The college offers five engineering branches: Civil Engineering 🏗️, Computer Science & Engineering 💻, Computer Science & Engineering with a specialization in Data Science 📊, Electrical Engineering⚡ Mechanical Engineering ⚙️, and Robotics & Automation 🤖. Each of these branches boasts a specific intake capacity, with a total of 360 students admitted annuallyThe college prides itself on its highly trained faculty, with many of the staff being alumni of prestigious institutions like IITs and NITs, ensuring that students receive education from some of the brightest minds in the country. GEC Arwal has quickly gained a reputation for its focus on fostering innovation and entrepreneurship, being ranked first among Bihar's institutions for startups.

Departments

Civil

🛤️Civil Engineering, with 60 seats, focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure such as roads, bridges, and buildings. Students learn to manage large-scale projects and develop sustainable solutions for urban planning, transportation, and water management systems.

Robotics

🤖Focused on designing, building, and controlling robotic systems, Robotics & Automation Engineering (with 30 seats) prepares students for cutting-edge advancements in automation, AI, and industrial robotics. The program combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, shaping the future of smart machines.

Mechanical

🛠️Mechanical Engineering (with 60 seats) provides students with a comprehensive understanding of design, manufacturing, and thermodynamics, preparing them for careers in industries like automotive, aerospace, and industrial machinery.

CSE

🖥️CSE offers a deep dive into programming, algorithms, and system architecture, equipping 60 students with skills for software development, cybersecurity, AI, and technology-driven industries. Students learn to innovate and create software solutions that drive the digital age.

CSE(DS)

📊This program integrates computer science with data analysis, teaching 60 students to interpret large datasets, develop AI models, and drive data-driven decision-making across industries. Graduates become experts in machine learning, big data, and predictive analytics.

Electrical

🔋With 120 seats, Electrical Engineering focuses on electrical systems, covering topics like power generation, electronics, and control systems. It shapes experts for industries like energy, telecommunications, and manufacturing, exploring the design and application of electrical technologies.
